About LifeFlow

Be the Reason Someone Lives — here’s how the journey works.

1. Donor Registration

Donors sign up with basic information, blood group, and contact details. An OTP-based login keeps the process simple and secure.

↓

2. Hospital Raises Request

Hospitals submit blood requests specifying the blood group, component type (Plasma, Platelets, or Whole Blood), units needed, and priority level.

↓

3. Admin Matches Donor

The admin reviews incoming requests, searches for compatible donors in the system, and allocates the right donor to each request.

↓

4. Donor Accepts & Donates

The matched donor receives a notification, reviews the request details, and confirms their availability. They then visit the hospital to donate.

↓

5. Request Fulfilled

Once the donation is complete, the request is marked as fulfilled. The entire journey is tracked and visible to all stakeholders through their dashboards.
